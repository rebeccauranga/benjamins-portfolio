Potros FC

Designs for the soccer club founded by my family.

First Logo and Uniform

Potros FC started as a recreational youth soccer team. The first logo and uniform were simple but still gave the team an identity.

Transitioning into an amateur club

After my brother and I no longer qualified to play youth soccer, we decided to make Potros FC into a serious club to compete in the Atlanta District Amateur Soccer League (ADASL). In this transition, we decided to rebrand.

Expansion

After a successful first season, we wanted to expand as a club by holding tryouts and obtaining sponsors for the club.

Customized Uniforms

To have a stronger sense of identity for the club, we thought it would be beneficial to have unique uniforms from the rest of the clubs in Atlanta. I designed a few concepts before making a final decision to be produced by Icarus FC, a company that prints custom soccer uniforms.



We made a final decision for a white jersey, black jersey, and a training jersey to be sent to Icarus FC to be printed. Here are the final uniforms.
