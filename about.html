About me

My name is Benjamin Uranga, and I’m an Interactive Design major with a minor in Technical Communications at Kennesaw State University. My two favorite activities are playing soccer and creating things. The feeling of having an idea then bringing that idea to life is unmatched. There is no better satisfaction than watching something I’ve envisioned being put together piece by piece. I started designing in 2015, where I fell in love with this process. Since then, I’ve taken what I’ve done and learned from all my mistakes and accomplishments in design. Now, I’m seeking to improve upon my skills as a designer and thinker. I wish to learn more about user experience and usability, while also strengthening my design capabilities.
